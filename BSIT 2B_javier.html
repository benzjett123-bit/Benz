Our Menu

Chicken

A delicious grilled chicken breast marinated in a blend of Mediterranean herbs, lemon, and olive oil. Served with a side of saffron rice and seasonal roasted vegetables. The chicken is slow-cooked to maintain maximum moisture and tenderness, then finished on a searing grill to lock in the flavor and provide a perfect crust. This dish is low in fat, high in protein, and is the perfect option for a healthy, satisfying meal. Our specialty is the herb butter reduction drizzled over the top just before serving. We use free-range chicken sourced from local farms.

* **Texture:** Tender and moist meat with a slightly crispy skin. * **Flavor Profile:** Zesty, herbaceous, and savory with a hint of smoky char.



















* **Allergens:** Contains dairy (butter in reduction).

Beef

Our signature slow-braised short ribs. These ribs are seasoned with a dry rub of smoked paprika, brown sugar, and coffee grounds, then slow-braised in a rich Pinot Noir reduction for eight hours until they are fork-tender. The ribs are served atop a creamy, truffle-infused polenta. The complex flavors of the red wine and coffee create a deep, umami-rich experience. This is a hearty and indulgent dish, perfect for a special occasion. We recommend pairing it with a full-bodied Cabernet Sauvignon.

* **Texture:** Fall-off-the-bone tenderness, creamy polenta. * **Flavor Profile:** Rich, smoky, savory, with notes of dark fruit and earthy truffle.



















* **Preparation Time:** Requires 45 minutes from order to table.

Sushi

The Dragon's Breath Roll: a specialty spicy tuna and avocado roll topped with eel sauce, crispy tempura flakes, and a micro cilantro garnish. The tuna is sustainably sourced and hand-cut daily. We blend sriracha with a house-made mayonnaise for the perfect level of heat, balancing it with the sweetness of the rich eel sauce. Served with fresh ginger and wasabi. This roll offers a perfect blend of fresh, crunchy, soft, and spicy elements.

* **Texture:** Soft rice, smooth tuna, creamy avocado, crunchy tempura. * **Flavor Profile:** Spicy heat, sweet umami, and refreshing raw fish flavor.



















* **Note:** Can be modified to be fully vegetarian upon request.
